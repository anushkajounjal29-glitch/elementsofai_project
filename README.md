# AI Career Guidance and Placement Assistant

**Final project for the Building AI course**

---

## Summary

AI Career Guidance and Placement Assistant is an AI-powered platform that helps college students identify suitable career paths, improve their skills, and prepare for placements. The system analyzes a student's skills, interests, and academic performance to provide personalized career recommendations and learning roadmaps.

**Building AI course project**

---

## Background

Many students complete their graduation without knowing which career is best for them. They often struggle to choose between software development, data analytics, AI, cybersecurity, cloud computing, government jobs, or higher education.

Common problems include:

* Lack of proper career guidance.
* Confusion about required technical skills.
* Difficulty preparing for campus placements.
* Limited awareness of industry requirements.
* No personalized learning roadmap.

I chose this project because many students, including myself, face uncertainty about career choices and placement preparation. An AI-based assistant can help students make informed decisions and improve their chances of getting a job.

---

## How is it used?

Students register on the platform and enter information such as:

* Education
* Skills
* Programming languages
* Interests
* Career goals
* Academic performance

The AI system analyzes the information and provides:

* Best career recommendations
* Skill gap analysis
* Placement preparation roadmap
* Recommended online courses
* Resume improvement suggestions
* Interview preparation tips

Users:

* College students
* Fresh graduates
* Placement cells
* Career counselors

The platform can be accessed through a website or mobile application.

## Challenges

The system has some limitations:

* Career recommendations depend on the quality of user data.
* AI cannot fully understand every individual's personal circumstances.
* Job market trends change frequently.
* Recommendations should not replace professional career counseling.
* Privacy and data security must be maintained.
